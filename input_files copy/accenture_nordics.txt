About the job
We are seeking a motivated and innovative Junior Applied AI Automation Engineer to join our Song Data & AI team. In this role, you will contribute to the development of impactful AI-driven automations and experiences. You will transform business concepts into scalable, production-ready building blocks and prototypes that integrate seamlessly into larger systems. This is an exciting opportunity to work at the intersection of AI and automation, leveraging cutting-edge technologies to deliver meaningful solutions.

Key Responsibilities:

Design and integrate APIs into automation workflows.
Develop, debug, and test lightweight services using Python or TypeScript.
Utilize SDKs to interact with language models and manage JSON payloads.
Work with embeddings, vector databases, and execute small-scale database queries.
Contribute to evaluation, iteration, instrumentation, and documentation processes.
Provide support for demos and internal workshops to showcase AI capabilities.

Basic Qualifications:

Approximately 1 year of coding experience (Python preferred).
Foundational understanding of large language models (LLMs) and embeddings, with hands-on experience.
Proficiency with Git, local development environments, and cloud CLI tools.
Familiarity with AI-assisted coding tools (e.g., Claude Code, Lvable, Cursor, Codex).

Preferred Qualifications:

Experience with orchestration tools such as n8n or LangChain.
Knowledge of Slack or Microsoft Teams integration.
Understanding of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) concepts and prompt engineering fundamentals.

Professional Skills / Behaviors:

A curious and exploratory mindset with a focus on craft, even in prototype development.
Strong team collaboration skills and a commitment to shared success.
Proficiency in English is required. Swedish is optional but an advantage.
